Recipe from Avinash Vaishnav

Ingredients:

 1. 1 lb ground beef
 2. Salt and pepper
 3. 4 hamburger buns
 4. Cheese slices (optional)
 5. Lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles
 6. Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise
 
Instructions:

  1. Make Patties: Season beef with salt and pepper. Form 4 patties.
  2. Cook Patties: Grill or pan-fry 3-4 minutes per side. Add cheese if desired.
  3. Toast Buns: Lightly toast buns on grill or in toaster.
  4. Assemble: Spread condiments on buns. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and patties.
  5. Serve: Enjoy hot with sides.
  
  Enjoy your burger!
